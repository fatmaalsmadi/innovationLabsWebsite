Mission & Vision

The Clubhouse mission is to provide a creative and safe out-of-school learning environment where young people from underserved communities work with adult mentors to exploretheir own ideas, develop new skills, and build confidence in themselves through the use of technology. Learn more.Learn more

Clubhouse History

The Clubhouse Network has a rich history extending back to our
beginnings at the Computer Museum in 1993. We’ve since
expanded across the globe to more than 100 Clubhouses in 20 countries serving over 25,000 youth. Explore our
history using our interactive timeline.Exploreour history using our interactive timeline.
