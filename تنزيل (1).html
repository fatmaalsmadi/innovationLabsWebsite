Your contribution provides teens with creative, inclusive, and safe spaces to explore, create, connect, and become their best selves.

Every dollar helps! Here are examples of what your donation helps fund:

$100

Send a Clubhouse youth on a Clubhouse-to-College/Career field trip

$250

Sponsor a one-hour Clubhouse STEM workshop

$750

Support a Clubhouse for a day

$1000

Send a youth to the international Teen Summit in Boston, MA

To pay by check, mail to: The Clubhouse Network, 2101 Washington St. Roxbury, MA 02119.

PayPal protects your financial information with industry-leading security and fraud prevention systems. A PayPal account is not required to donate; a valid credit or debit card may be used. Once your payment is complete, you will be emailed a receipt for this tax-deductible transaction.
